# Job-Form-filler
Easy Extension to fill Google Forms, you can tweak this to make your own form filler.


Load the Extension into Chrome
Create a folder locally (Autofill-Extension), paste these files into the folder, and edit with your content. 
Now you will install your custom extension into your browser.

Open Google Chrome.

In the URL bar, type: chrome://extensions/ and hit Enter.

In the top right corner, toggle on "Developer mode".

In the top left, click the "Load unpacked" button.

Select the Autofill-Extension folder you created in Step 1.


Your extension is now installed! You will see it in your list of extensions. Pin it to your toolbar by clicking the puzzle piece icon in Chrome and clicking the pin next to "Job Autofill Pro".
